What is Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel perfect for aerial photography. The drone has all the best DJI technology, it will transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. The L1D-20c features Hasselblad's unique Natural Color Solution (HNCS)5 technology, allowing users to capture stunning aerial shots with 20 megapixel resolution and stunning colors.

Characteristics

Dlog-M 10 bit

Mavic 2 Pro supports Dlog-M 10-bit color profile with a wider dynamic range for more color grading options.

Hasselblad Camera

Camera Hasselblad L1D-20C known for ergonomic design and excellent image quality.

CMOS 1 sensor

New 1-inch CMOS sensor has four times the active area of the Mavic Pro

HDR video

With support for 4K HDR 10bit video, the Mavic 2 Pro can be connected to an HLG 4K compatible TV and viewed in full color.
